Hours of video is created every minute. It is difficult to decide whether to watch a video or not based on thumbnail image, or rating or number of likes. If the video is long, chances of watching it is less even though it is good rated. Using SnipInterest we can

  • decide whether to watch a video or not
  • watch interested parts of video
  • shows interest map
  • bookmark videos with interval
  • create and watch sniplists

Why SnipInterest?

If video is long or boring, we randomly click on timeline and look for interesting and good video. In video timeline Interest map shows the best parts of video. After watching few videos we feel it's not worth watching them even though they are good rated.

Even less rated videos may have some interesting parts.

Snip - A snip is a video interval with Beginning and Ending time.It can be entire video with Interval 0sec to end(full time).

Sniplist - Sniplist contains one or more snips in an order to play them sequentially. Instead of cropping and combining one or more videos into one, we can easily create sniplists.

Logo

Logo indicates the interest map from light to dark colors. It can have colors similar to heat map
