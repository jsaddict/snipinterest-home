Hours of video is created every minute. It is difficult to decide whether to watch a video or not based on thumbnail image, or rating or number of likes. Most of the videos are good rated in popular video sharing websites. If the video is long, chances of watching it is less even though it is good rated. Using SnipInterest we can

  • decide whether to watch a video or not
  • watch interested parts of video
  • shows interest map
  • bookmark videos with interval
  • create and watch sniplists

Why SnipInterest?

After watching few videos we feel it's not worth watching them even though they are good rated.

If a video is long or boring, we randomly click on timeline and look for good video.

Even less rated videos may have some interesting parts.

To simplify this SnipInterest shows the Interest map in video timeline.

Watch the best and relevant video in less time.

Snip - A snip is a video interval with Beginning and Ending time.It can be entire video with Interval 0 sec to end(full time).

Sniplist - Sniplist contains one or more snips in an order to play them sequentially. Instead of cropping and combining one or more videos into one, we can easily create sniplists.

Logo

Logo indicates the interest map from light to dark colors. It can have colors similar to heat map
